Sanad — A voice-first assistant that makes life simpler for the elderly

Talk naturally in Arabic. Sanad listens, understands, and helps with reminders, payments, and calling trusted contacts — no complicated menus or small buttons.

See demo How it works
  • ✅ Arabic (Saudi) speech recognition
  • ✅ Natural TTS voices

  • ✅ Lightweight, offline-first UX options
For hackathon/demo: use the Speech Studio playground or our lightweight demo app. Full deployment requires a Pay-As-You-Go Azure subscription.

How it works — simple 3-step flow

1. Speak

User talks naturally in Arabic — no menus or typing.

2. Sanad listens

Speech-to-text converts voice to text and extracts intent.

3. Respond

Sanad replies with a natural Arabic voice and performs actions.

Key features

Voice-first UX

Large text, simple confirmations, and voice prompts to avoid confusion.

Reminders & daily help

Medication, appointments, and bill reminders with simple voice confirmations.

Trusted contacts

Call or message family/caregivers with a single voice command.

Privacy-first

Local caching, minimal telemetry, and explicit permission prompts.
